Who is Who?


MEMBERS OF THE RED ARMY FACTION (RAF)
Mieko Toyama (Maki Sakai)

Died on January 7, 1972 at the age of 25.

Tsuneo Mori (Go Jibiki)

Committed suicide on January 1, 1973 in Tokyo Prison at the age of 28.

Kunio Bando (Shima Onishi)

Arrested on February 28, 1972 as riot police broke into Asama
Mountain Lodge to free the held hostage lodge keeper.
On August 4, 1975, the Japanese Red Army (JRA) took over
the American Embassy in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, securing
the release of five people from prison in Japan including
Kunio Bando.

Ryuzaburo Shindo (Keigo Kasuya)

Died on January 1, 1972 at the age of 21 after he was lynched.

Takaya Shiomi (Taku Sakaguchi)

On March 15, 1970, Takaya Shiomi, leader of the RAF,
was arrested.

Fusako Shigenobu (Anri Ban)

On February 28, 1971, Fusako Shigenobu, in charge of “international
development”, escaped to Lebanon. After the Asama Lodge incident,
she became the only leader and founded the Japanese Red Army
(JRA). For thirty years, with terrorist factions from all territories,
she organized spectacular attacks and hostage takings on all
continents. She is arrested on November 8, 2000 in Osaka.
On April 14, 2000, Shigenobu declared the dissolution of JRA.


MEMBERS OF THE REVOLUTIONARY LEFT FACTION (RLF)

 Hiroshi Sakaguchi (ARATA)

Arrested on February 28, 1972 as riot police broke into Asama
Mountain Lodge to free the held hostage lodge keeper.
On June 16, 1982, Hirsohi Sakaguchi received the death penalty.
Currently in prison, on death row.

Hiroko Nagata (Akie Namiki)

In January 1971, Tsuneo Mori (RAF) made contact with her, as
the leader of the RLF. On July 15, 1971, they merged, becoming the Unified Red Army, then changing their name to the United Red Army (URA).
On Februrary 17, 1972, she and Tsuneo Mori were arrested.
On June 16, 1982, Hiroko Nagata received the death penalty.
Currently in prison, on death row.

Masakuni Yoshino (Takagi Uda)

Arrested on February 28, 1972 as riot police broke into Asama
Mountain Lodge to free the held hostage lodge keeper.
He is now serving life sentence.
He was the father of Michiyo Kaneko’s child (she died while she
was eight months pregnant).

Koichi Teraoka (Yugo Saso)

Executed on January 18, 1972 at the age of 24.
He wasn’t lynched: Tsuneo Mori passed the death sentence.

Setsuko Otsuki (Yuki Fujii)

Died on January 30, 1972 at the age of 23.

Michiyo Kaneko (Maria Abe)

On February 4, 1972, Michiyo Kaneko, eight months pregnant with Masakuni Yoshino’s child, died at the age of 23.

Yoshitaka Kato (Hassei Takano)

Died on January 4, 1972 at the age of 22.

Michinori Kato  (Toshimitsu Kokido)

Arrested on February 28, 1972 as riot police broke into Asama
Mountain Lodge to free the held hostage lodge keeper.

Motohisa Kato (Soran Tamoto)

Arrested on February 28, 1972 as riot police broke into Asama
Mountain Lodge to free the held hostage lodge keeper.
He was held as a minor as he was 17 at time of arrest.
